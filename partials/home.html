Meet the Artist

My art style is largely Impressionistic: bold colors and brushstrokes. Long before I started painting I loved to look at paintings! It started with visiting museums in Europe during trips abroad, where painters such as Van Gogh, Gaugin, Monet and other Impressionists fascinated me. I drank in their bold colors and brushstrokes thick with paint. Later the Swedish artist Birger Sandzen added to my fascination with color and bold brush strokes layered on in impasto...
